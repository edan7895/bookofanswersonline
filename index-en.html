The Origin of The Book of Answers

The Book of Answers was first created by Carol Bolt, an artist and author known for blending creativity with intuitive wisdom. Her idea was simple yet profound — a book that could respond to your questions with short, poetic, and thought-provoking answers. Since its first publication, it has inspired millions around the world to pause, reflect, and trust their inner voice. Today, the Book of Answers Online continues this legacy by bringing that same magic to your screen, offering instant access to guidance and inspiration wherever you are.
