The Origin of The Book of Answers

The Book of Answers was created by Carol Bolt, an artist and author known for blending creativity with quiet insight. Her idea was beautifully simple — a book that could respond to questions with short, poetic, and meaningful phrases. Since its first publication, it has inspired readers across the world to pause, reflect, and trust their intuition. Today, the Book of Answers Online carries this timeless idea into the digital world, offering instant access to reflection and inspiration anytime, anywhere.
